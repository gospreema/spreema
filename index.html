The E-Commerce Challenge

Today’s online shopping experience is broken. Here’s why:

Search Struggles

45% of customers face poor search functionality, with 40% overwhelmed by options and 35% confused by filters, driving frustration and lost sales.


  • 45% - Poor Search Functionality
  • 40% - Overwhelming Options
  • 35% - Confusing Filters
  • 30% - Slow Performance
  • 25% - Inconsistent Categorization
  • 20% - Poor Product Info
  • 15% - Keyword Mismatches

Checkout Dropoff

Over 70% of carts are abandoned. 66% cite shipping costs, 14% are missing guest checkout, and the process is too complex for 11%.


  • 66% - Shipping Costs
  • 41% - Not Ready to Purchase
  • 25% - Higher Price and Fees
  • 24% - Save for Later
  • 14% - No Guest Checkout
  • 12% - Asks too much
  • 11% - Complex Process

Costly Returns

Returns exceed $550B yearly, with 50% from wrong sizes, 42% by unmet expectations, and we have a 10% to 20% chance of receiving counterfeits.


  • 52% - Damaged or Defective
  • 50% - Wrong Size or Fit
  • 42% - Bad Photo or Expectations
  • 37% - Incorrect Item
  • 20% - Changed Mind
  • 10% - Poor Quality or Low Value
  • 6% - Customer Order Error

* All problems listed above will be addressed, most eliminated completely, all materially improved.

** Will even reduce how often customers change their minds. Ask us how.
